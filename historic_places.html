Historic Places of Assam

Explore Assam's Rich Heritage

Assam, with its rich history, is home to several historic places that offer a glimpse into its glorious past. From ancient temples to majestic forts, these sites reflect the architectural and cultural heritage of Assam. Notable historic places include the Kamakhya Temple, known for its unique Shakti worship, the ancient ruins of the Ahom kingdom, and the majestic Rang Ghar, a historic amphitheater. Each site tells a story of the land's rich history and diverse cultures. Explore these historic treasures to uncover the fascinating history of Assam.

Sivasagar

The former capital of the Ahom Kingdom, Sivasagar is rich in history. It features the famous Sivasagar Tank and several Ahom-era temples like Shivadol, Vishnudol, and Devidol. The town is a testament to the architectural and cultural achievements of the Ahom dynasty.

Kareng Ghar

Kareng Ghar, situated in Rangpur, was once the royal palace of the Ahom kings. The architectural style of Kareng Ghar reflects the grandeur of the Ahom dynasty. It is an important historical site that offers insights into the royal lifestyle and governance of the Ahom era.

Agnigarh

Situated in Tezpur, Agnigarh is a historic hillock with a fort that dates back to ancient times. It is associated with the legend of Princess Usha and Prince Aniruddha, adding mythological significance to its historical value.
