Traditional Dresses of Jammu and Kashmir

Discover the Traditional Attire of Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir's traditional attire is a reflection of its rich cultural heritage, deeply influenced by the region's climate and history. The most iconic garment is the **Pheran**, a long robe worn by both men and women, designed to keep warm in the cold climate. Women often pair the Pheran with **Taranga**, a traditional headdress, while men may wear it with a skullcap or a turban. These garments are not just clothing but symbols of Jammu and Kashmir's unique cultural identity and craftsmanship.

Famous Traditional Dresses

Pheran

The Pheran is a traditional long robe worn by both men and women, essential for the cold climate of the region.

Taranga

Taranga is a traditional headdress worn by Kashmiri women, especially on special occasions and during weddings.

Kashmiri Shawl

The Kashmiri Shawl, known for its intricate embroidery and fine fabric, is a symbol of elegance and tradition.
