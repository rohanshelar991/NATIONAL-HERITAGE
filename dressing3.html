Traditional Dresses of Rajasthan

Discover the Traditional Attire of Rajasthan

The traditional attire of Rajasthan is a vibrant reflection of the state's rich cultural heritage, characterized by its vivid colors, intricate designs, and practical utility, suitable for the region's climate.

Famous Traditional Dresses

Rajputi Poshak

The Rajputi Poshak is a traditional outfit worn by Rajput women, comprising a Ghagra, Choli, and Odhni. It is known for its regal appearance, with heavy embroidery, zari work, and intricate designs.

Safa

Safa is a type of turban worn by men in Rajasthan, particularly during weddings and festivals. Unlike the Pagri, which is simpler, the Safa is more elaborate and is often made from richly colored fabric with detailed patterns.

Angrakha

The Angrakha is a traditional upper garment worn by Rajasthani men. It is typically made from cotton or silk and is tied at the side with strings.
