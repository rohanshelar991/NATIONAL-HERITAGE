Traditional Dresses of Kerala

Discover the Traditional Attire of Kerala

Kerala's traditional attire is a reflection of its rich cultural heritage. The most iconic garment is the Kasavu Saree, worn by women, especially during festivals like Onam. It is known for its elegant white color and golden border. Men traditionally wear the Mundu, a garment similar to a dhoti, often paired with a shirt or a traditional Kurta. These garments are not just clothing but symbols of Kerala's deep-rooted traditions and simplicity.

Famous Traditional Dresses

Kasavu Saree

The Kasavu Saree is a traditional attire for women in Kerala, characterized by its pristine white fabric and golden border.

Mundu

The Mundu is a traditional garment for men, usually paired with a shirt, and is a symbol of Kerala's simplicity and tradition.

Set Mundu

The Set Mundu is another popular attire for women, often worn during festivals and ceremonies, featuring a two-piece garment with a golden border.
