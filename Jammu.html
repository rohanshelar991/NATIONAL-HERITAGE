Jammu and Kashmir: The Paradise on Earth

Welcome to Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir, located in the northernmost part of India, is known for its breathtaking landscapes, pristine valleys, and rich cultural heritage. Often referred to as "Heaven on Earth," this region is a treasure trove of natural beauty and vibrant traditions. The culture of Jammu and Kashmir is a unique blend of Hindu, Buddhist, and Islamic influences, reflected in its music, dance, cuisine, and handicrafts. Whether it's the mesmerizing beauty of Dal Lake, the traditional art of Pashmina weaving, or the rhythm of Rouf dance, Jammu and Kashmir offer a magical experience for every visitor. Discover the diverse and vibrant traditions that define this enchanting region.

Key Highlights

Shikara Ride

Experience the serene beauty of Dal Lake with a traditional Shikara ride, a must-do activity in Srinagar.

Rouf Dance

Rouf is a traditional folk dance performed during festivals and celebrations, showcasing the vibrant culture of Kashmir.

Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a popular hill station and skiing destination, known for its stunning snow-covered landscapes.
