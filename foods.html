Famous Foods of Assam

Welcome to Assam's Culinary World

Assam offers a variety of delicious and unique dishes, from the simple rice and fish curries to the elaborate preparations of duck meat and bamboo shoot pickles. Dive into the flavors of Assam and discover recipes that have been passed down through generations.

Famous Foods

Masor Tenga

Masor Tenga is a tangy fish curry that is light, flavorful, and a favorite in Assamese households.

Aloo Pitika

Aloo Pitika is a simple, comforting mashed potato dish that pairs perfectly with rice and dal.

Pithas

Pithas are traditional Assamese rice cakes, often enjoyed during Bihu, made with rice flour and filled with coconut or jaggery.
