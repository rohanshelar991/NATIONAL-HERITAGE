Traditional Dresses of Gujarat

Discover the Traditional Attire of Gujarat

Gujarati traditional dress for men is a Dhoti and Kurta, always accompanied by a Phento (kind of turban). The tribal men are also seen wearing the same. Oxidized jewels resonate with the state of Gujarat.The women are seen gracefully adorning themselves in Chaniya Cholis, commonly called Ghagra or Lehenga Cholis.

Famous Traditional Dresses

Chaniya Choli

Traditional Dress of Gujarat. The traditional attire of Gujarati women is Chaniya Choli or Ghagra Choli; Women also wear an Odhni (dupatta or chunni) with it.

Raas Garba

Raas is a form of folk dance done in pairs. It is a dance done to reenact the Raas Leela or the dance of Lord Krishna.

Kediyu

Kediyu is a garment that is worn above the chorno to cover the top part of the body. A kediyu is frock type kurta with frills, worn by the men in Gujarat.
