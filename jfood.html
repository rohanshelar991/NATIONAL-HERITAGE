Famous Foods of Jammu and Kashmir

Welcome to Jammu and Kashmir's Culinary World

Jammu and Kashmir are known for their rich and flavorful cuisine, featuring dishes infused with aromatic spices, saffron, and dried fruits. Experience the unique tastes of Kashmiri cuisine and discover recipes that have been cherished for generations.

Famous Foods

Rogan Josh

Rogan Josh is a signature dish of Kashmiri cuisine, a slow-cooked lamb curry made with yogurt, aromatic spices, and a rich red color from Kashmiri red chilies.

Yakhni

Yakhni is a delicate yogurt-based curry, often made with lamb or chicken, flavored with saffron, cardamom, and other aromatic spices.

Wazwan

Wazwan is a traditional multi-course feast in Kashmiri cuisine, featuring a variety of dishes including kebabs, curries, and rice, reflecting the region's rich culinary heritage.
