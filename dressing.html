Traditional Dresses of Assam

Discover the Traditional Attire of Assam

Assam's traditional attire is a reflection of its rich cultural heritage. The most famous garment is the Mekhela Chador, worn by Assamese women. It's an elegant two-piece outfit, intricately woven with traditional motifs, often in Assam silk. The men wear Dhoti and Gamosa, with the latter being a symbol of Assamese pride. The vibrant colors and intricate designs of these garments reflect the essence of Assam's cultural diversity.

Famous Traditional Dresses

Mekhela Chador

A traditional Assamese garment for women, the Mekhela Chador is known for its elegance and intricate designs, often made from Assam silk.

Gamosa

A symbol of Assamese culture, the Gamosa is a rectangular piece of cloth, often gifted as a sign of respect and honor.

Dhoti Kurta

Worn by Assamese men, the Dhoti Kurta is a traditional outfit that reflects the simplicity and cultural essence of Assam.
