Traditional Dance Forms of Gujarat

Explore Gujarat's Dance Forms

Dating back to the ancient era of Krishna, singing and dancing with 'Flute' and other Instruments with the dance forms like Garba, Garbi and Raas is the most popular Dance celebrations in Gujarat. A legendary unique Folk Dance form also has variations with Dandia or stick Raas.

Traditional Dance Forms

Garba

The Garba is Gujarat's famous folk dance.Garba is a form of Gujarati dance which originates from the state of Gujarat, India. The name is derived from the Sanskrit term Garbha .

Tippani

STamasha is a lively folk theater form from Maharashtra, blending music, dance, and drama, often performed in an open-air setting.

Dandiya Raas

Raas or Dandiya Raas is the socio-religious folk dance originating from Indian state of Gujarat and popularly performed in the festival of Navaratri.
