Gujarat: A Flourishing State with Cultural Diversity

Welcome to Gujarat

Gujarat is a state located on the western coast of India, bordered by the Arabian Sea to the west and the Indian states of Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, and Maharashtra to the north, east, and south, respectively.The predominant language spoken in Gujarat is Gujarati. It is known for its rich history, diverse culture, and economic significance.The capital of Gujarat is Gandhinagar, while its largest city is Ahmedabad. Other notable cities include Surat, Vadodara, and Rajkot.Gujarat is one of India’s most industrialized states and is a major hub for trade and commerce. The state's economy is driven by industries such as textiles, chemicals, petrochemicals, and engineering. The port city of Kandla is a crucial economic and trade center.

Key Highlights

Navratri Festival

Navratri is a festival of nine nights and on the tenth day, Goddess Durga, who is worshipped throughout the nine days, is immersed in holy water after performing a pooja.

Patola Silk

Gujarat is known for its world famous Patola Sarees.Patola sarees are extremely vibrant due to the use of natural dyes in the making of the fabric.

Pirotan Island

Pirotan Island is an island in the Marine National Park, Arabian Sea. It is located 12 nautical miles (22 km) off the coast (Bedi Port), Jamnagar District of Gujarat state, India.
