Traditional Dance Forms

Kathakali Dance

Kathakali is a classical dance-drama known for its elaborate costumes, makeup, and expressive movements, often depicting stories from Indian epics.

Mohiniyattam Dance

Mohiniyattam is a graceful dance form performed by women, characterized by its soft, fluid movements and themes of love and devotion.

Theyyam Dance

Theyyam is a ritualistic dance form performed in temples, where dancers embody gods and spirits, creating a powerful visual spectacle.

Koodiyattam Dance

Koodiyattam is an ancient Sanskrit theater tradition, where dance and drama are combined in a highly stylized form, recognized as a UNESCO heritage.
