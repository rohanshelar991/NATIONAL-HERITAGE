Famous Foods of Kerala

Welcome to Kerala's Culinary World

Kerala is known for its diverse and flavorful cuisine, featuring dishes rich in coconut, spices, and fresh seafood. Experience the unique tastes of Kerala and learn about recipes that have been cherished for generations.

Famous Foods

Appam

Appam is a soft and fluffy pancake made from fermented rice batter, often served with stew or coconut milk.

Kerala Sadhya

Kerala Sadhya is a traditional feast served on a banana leaf, featuring a variety of vegetarian dishes, pickles, and payasam.

Fish Moilee

Fish Moilee is a mild and creamy fish curry made with coconut milk, turmeric, and green chilies, best enjoyed with rice.
