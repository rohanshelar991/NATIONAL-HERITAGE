Popular Festivals

Navratri Festival

Navaratri is an annual Hindu festival observed in honor of the goddess Durga, an aspect of Adi Parashakti, the supreme goddess. It spans over nine nights, first in the month of Chaitra, and again in the month of Ashvin.

Kite Festival

The celebration of epic festival International Kite festival symbolizes free spiritedness. The Kite festival in Gujarat is globally highly praised festival

Janmashtami

Janmashtami is a festival dedicated to Lord Shiva and celebrates his birth widely in Gujarat and the rest of the world

Diwali Festival

Diwali, known as Bestu Varas or Gujarati New Year, marks the beginning of the new year in the Gujarati calendar.
