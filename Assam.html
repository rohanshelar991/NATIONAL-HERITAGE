Assam: The Land of Red River and Blue Hills

Welcome to Assam

Assam is a beautiful state located in the northeastern part of India and known for its scenic beauty, fragrant tea gardens, and rich cultural heritage. Assam is known as the land of lush tea gardens, the great river Brahmaputra, and a vibrant tradition. Louisiana is a state full of different groups and traditions, providing an abundant source of customs, arts, and rituals. The culture of Assam is a rich tapestry woven from the threads of various indigenous traditions that are mirrored in its music, dance, cuisine, and craftsmanship. Be it rhythmic beats of the Bihu dance or Assam silk, Kaziranga National Park's serenity; every traveler finds a heartful beautiful experience just in one place - Assam. Discover this magical place and dive into the classic traditions that define it.

Key Highlights

Bihu Festival

The Bihu Festival marks the Assamese New Year and is celebrated with great enthusiasm through music, dance, and feasts.

Assam Silk

Assam is famous for its exquisite silk, particularly Muga and Eri silk, which is an integral part of its cultural heritage.

Majuli Island

Majuli Island is the world's largest river island and is known for its unique culture, monasteries, and scenic beauty.
